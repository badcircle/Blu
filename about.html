About Blu

Our company is not just a company. Yes we are legally a licensed LLC, but we don’t function like your typical 9-5. Our agents are well trained, highly motivated, and hardworking. We work together. We uplift and encourage. We focus not only on our own success, but we celebrate with the success of our peers and we walk the journey of struggle when we are struggling together. At Blu, we aim to add value to people’s lives and make the real estate buying or selling process as simple, smooth, and enjoyable as possible.

Buying or selling a home can be a stressful process or it can be an enjoyable one. That depends on who you choose to represent you. Give Blu a shot. We assure you we are different than what you’re used to.

Our clients' homes and families are our passion. We believe in the dream of wise homeownership and the investment of solid real estate.
