It's okay to be different

Blu is a full-service real estate brokerage representing clients from a variety of backgrounds with a variety of goals. From single-family residential, condos, duplexes, new construction, commercial, to luxury real estate, Blu offers exceptional service in all areas. Blu was founded on the idea that Realtors should be problem solvers instead of problems to solve. Likewise Blu’s vision is to always be forward-thinking instead of reactionary. In all the things we do we are just a little bit different, and we think that’s ok!

Shunning the traditional model of real estate business, Blu harbors an environment of teamwork, collaboration, and creativity. The days of cut-throat real estate where agents must watch their backs lest they lose a client to a competitor in the office are in the rear view mirror at Blu. We provide our clients a strong competitive advantage because of our office cooperation. Blu is more than a company – we are a community. We value one another and we value personal excellence.

Blu offers services well beyond putting a sign in the yard or opening the door to a home. Whether our clients are looking to build new construction, remodel their kitchen and need design assistance, looking for trustworthy contractors to help with a room addition, or anything else real estate related, Blu agents are able to help!
